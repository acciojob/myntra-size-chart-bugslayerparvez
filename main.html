Size Selection Tips

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest, natural waistline, and from shoulder to wrist for sleeves.

Fit Preference

For a relaxed fit, select one size up. For a slim fit, stay with your regular size.

Fabric Consideration

Stretchy fabrics may allow for a more forgiving fit, while structured fabrics require precise measurements.
